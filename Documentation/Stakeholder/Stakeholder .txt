The stakeholders for AQAY:

1. Adminstration group The individuals or group of people who initiated the idea, invested in the platform, and have an ownership stake.

2. Local Brands owners: These are the businesses that list their products on the platform. 

3. Customers:The end-users who purchase products from the AQAY platform.

4. Employees: The people working for the platform, including developers, customer support, marketing, and other staff. Their skills and dedication are essential for the smooth operation of the platform.
5.Supervisors: individuals responsible for overseeing and managing the work of employees within a platform
